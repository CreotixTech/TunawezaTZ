Collaborating for Impact

At TunawezaTZ, we believe that sustainable community development happens through collaboration. Our local partnerships are the foundation of our work, allowing us to create meaningful impact in the communities we serve.

We work with a diverse range of Tanzanian organizations, government agencies, educational institutions, and community groups to deliver programs that address real needs and create lasting change.

Our Local Partners

We're proud to collaborate with these outstanding Tanzanian organizations

Education

Mwanza Education Initiative

Collaborating on digital literacy programs in secondary schools across the Mwanza region, providing access to technology education for underserved youth.

Government

Mwanza City Council

Working together on community development initiatives and youth employment programs that align with the city's development strategy.

NGO

Tanzania Youth Alliance

Joint programs focused on youth empowerment, leadership development, and creating pathways to employment for young Tanzanians.

Business

Lake Tech Solutions

Local technology company providing internship opportunities, mentorship, and employment pathways for our program graduates.

Education

University of Mwanza

Academic partnership providing curriculum development support, research collaboration, and access to university facilities for our programs.

Community

Nyakato Community Association

Grassroots collaboration to identify community needs and co-design programs that address local challenges through technology solutions.

Types of Partnerships

We offer various collaboration models to suit different organizational goals

Program Partnerships

Co-design and implementation of educational programs and community initiatives that align with shared goals and objectives.

Employment Partnerships

Provide internship opportunities, on-the-job training, and employment pathways for our program graduates.

Educational Partnerships

Collaborate on curriculum development, knowledge sharing, and access to educational resources and facilities.

Community Partnerships

Work with community organizations to identify needs and develop locally-relevant technology solutions.
