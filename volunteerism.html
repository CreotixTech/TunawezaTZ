Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about volunteering

Do I need prior teaching experience to volunteer?

While teaching experience is helpful, it's not always required. We provide training and support for all our volunteers. What's most important is your passion for our mission and willingness to learn.

What time commitment is expected?

It varies by role, but most opportunities require 2-8 hours per week. We offer both regular commitments and one-time volunteering opportunities to accommodate different schedules.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes, we have several remote volunteering opportunities, especially for mentoring, content creation, and virtual tutoring roles.

Do you accept international volunteers?

Yes, we welcome international volunteers, particularly for remote opportunities. For on-site volunteering, we can help with information about visas and accommodation but cannot provide financial support for travel expenses.
