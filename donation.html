Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about supporting our work

How is my donation used?

85% of your donation directly supports our programs, including training materials, equipment, and instructor costs. 10% covers administrative expenses, and 5% supports fundraising efforts to sustain our work.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, TunawezaTZ is a registered non-profit organization in Tanzania, and donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your donation that can be used for tax purposes.

Can I donate equipment instead of money?

Yes, we accept in-kind donations of technology equipment, software, and other resources. Please contact us to discuss what items you'd like to donate and we can coordinate the details.

How can I get updates on how my donation is used?

We send regular updates to our donors through our newsletter and impact reports. You can also follow us on social media to see daily updates on our programs and the students we're serving.
