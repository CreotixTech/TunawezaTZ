Our Story

TunawezaTZ was founded in 2018 with a vision to empower Tanzanian youth through technology education and digital skills training. What started as a small community initiative has grown into a impactful organization that has transformed hundreds of lives.

We believe that every young person in Tanzania deserves the opportunity to develop the skills needed to thrive in the digital economy. Our programs are designed to bridge the digital divide and create pathways to meaningful employment and entrepreneurship.

Through our holistic approach, we provide not just technical training but also mentorship, career guidance, and community support to ensure our participants' success.

Our Mission

To empower Tanzanian youth with digital skills, technology access, and career opportunities that enable them to thrive in the digital economy and become drivers of innovation in their communities.

Our Vision

We envision a Tanzania where all young people have equal access to digital education and opportunities, enabling them to become leaders in technology and creators of sustainable solutions for local and global challenges.

Our Values

Community

We believe in the power of community and collaboration to drive sustainable change and create opportunities for all.

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking and innovative approaches to solve complex challenges in education and technology access.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering high-quality programs and maintaining excellence in all aspects of our work.
